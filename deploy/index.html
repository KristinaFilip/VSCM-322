About Me

My name is Kristina Filip and I am currently a student at Drexel University studying Graphic Design. Collaboration, creativity, and problem solving are important parts of being a graphic designer that I value. I enjoy coming up with creative solutions to problems and always encourage myself to tackle challenging projects with these values in mind. I continuously strive to improve my graphic design skills and am willing to learn new things along the way. Outside of designing, I enjoy reading books and graphic novels, spending time with my family, and photography.
